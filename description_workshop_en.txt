Use the Commander's Baton, get rid of troubles, and lead the town NPCs to defeat all enemies in front of you!
You can craft this near a Demon Altar using 
king statues * 3, queen statues * 3,
love potions * 10, stink potions * 10,
and an advanced combat techniques.
Inspired by Red Alert 2.
Multiplayer mode is not supported and bugs may exist.
Have a good time :)
